## Tips for Melting Cheese Successfully

- **Temperature Control**: Use medium to medium-low heat to prevent scorching or uneven melting
- **Moisture Content**: Cheeses with higher moisture and fat content melt more smoothly
- **Shredding**: Shredded cheese melts faster and more evenly than large chunks
- **Combining Cheeses**: Mix high-melting cheeses with flavorful varieties for best results
- **Avoid Overheating**: Most cheeses break down and become greasy if overheated
