Master the Craft

Tips for the Perfect Melt

🌡️
Temperature Control

Use medium to medium-low heat to prevent scorching or uneven melting.

💧
Moisture Content

Cheeses with higher moisture and fat content melt more smoothly and evenly.

🧴
Shredding

Shredded cheese melts faster and more evenly than large chunks or slabs.

🎨
Combining Cheeses

Mix high-melting cheeses with flavorful varieties for best texture and taste.

🚫
Avoid Overheating

Most cheeses break down and become greasy if overheated — patience is key.

Put Them to Work

Signature Melt Recipes

🫕
Gruyère + Fontina
Classic Alpine Fondue
  • Rub pot with a halved garlic clove.
  • Warm dry white wine over medium-low heat until steaming.
  • Add shredded Gruyère and Fontina in small handfuls, stirring in figure-eights.
  • Season with nutmeg and white pepper. Serve immediately with crusty bread.
🍔
American + Aged Cheddar
The Perfect Smash Burger
  • Smash 80/20 beef balls on a ripping-hot cast iron.
  • Season generously with salt and pepper.
  • Layer one slice American, one slice cheddar while still on the heat.
  • Cover briefly to steam-melt; stack and serve immediately.
🫕
Raclette
Raclette Board
  • Arrange boiled baby potatoes, cornichons, and charcuterie on a board.
  • Melt thick slices of Raclette under a broiler or raclette grill.
  • Scrape molten cheese directly over the potatoes.
  • Finish with cracked black pepper and fresh thyme.
🍕
Whole Milk Mozzarella
Neapolitan Margherita
  • Stretch high-hydration dough thin; spoon on crushed San Marzano tomatoes.
  • Tear whole-milk mozzarella and scatter over the sauce.
  • Bake at maximum oven temperature (500°F+) for 6–8 minutes.
  • Finish with fresh basil and a drizzle of good olive oil.
